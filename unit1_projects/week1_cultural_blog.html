Week 1 - Values

When you think of the times in your life where you've been the happiest, the proudest, or the most satisfied, which of the following values come to mind?

I will scale the following values with a number rating to the right as to how I try to live up to them. Adventure(4), Community(4), Creativity(5), Effectiveness(5), Efficiency(5), Enthusiasm(5), Freedom(4), Growth(4), Influence(4), Leadership(3), Quality(4), Reputation(5), Self-respect(4).

Effeciency! I am a big fan of creating an enviornment where I can create and influence the flow of work so that I can determine a clear path to completion. Managing resources and establishing guidelines for success are important to me and line right up with being effecient.

The last topic that someone asked me my advice on was how to deal with an antagonistic presense in conversation.
